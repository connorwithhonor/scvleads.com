Questions You're Probably Asking

Straight answers. No fluff.

What if I already have an agency?
Ask them one question: "Can I log into the ad account and see everything?" If the answer is no or complicated, you're flying blind. We give you full access from day one.
What's the contract length?
Month-to-month after the initial 90-day runway. We earn your business every month. If we're not delivering, you leave. Simple.
How quickly will I see leads?
Most clients see their first leads within 7-14 days of campaign launch. Quality leads that convert? That's what Month 2-3 is for — optimization.
Do I need to be tech-savvy?
Nope. If you can use a smartphone, you can use HonorElevate. We handle all the technical setup. You just respond to leads.
What industries do you work with?
Home services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing), dental practices, real estate agents, mortgage professionals. If you need local leads, we can probably help.
What's the $1,500 setup fee for?
Building your custom campaign architecture, integrating your CRM, setting up tracking pixels/CAPI, creating initial ad creative, and configuring your HonorElevate platform. It's real work.
Can my whole team access the system?
Yes. Every employee can have their own login with the HonorElevate app (iOS, Android, desktop). Round-robin leads, individual notifications, performance tracking — all built in.
What if this isn't right for me?
We'll tell you on the strategy call. If you're not ready, don't have the ad budget, or we're not a fit — we say so. No hard sell. Ever.
